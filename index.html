Yours truly, a May baby born in year 1999. I am from Penang, Malaysia. It's certainly beutiful, isn't it? We basically have E-V-E-R-Y-T-H-I-N-G. By that I mean mouth-watering local food.

I love food since I was born to be an eater in Penang. The next thing that comes close to food is music. I used to play music, a lot. Rhythmic musical instrument is what I am talking about. Drum set, Marching drums and all sorts of percussions were my favourite. I love performing arts.
